MBTI Personality Test

Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)

1. Do you prefer to spend time in large groups or in smaller, more intimate settings?



2. After a busy week, do you feel energized by socializing or prefer to recharge alone?



Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)

3. Do you tend to focus on the details or the big picture?



4. Do you prefer to stick to tried-and-true methods, or do you enjoy exploring new ideas?



Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)

5. When making decisions, do you primarily rely on logic or your feelings?



6. In arguments, do you focus more on the facts or the emotional impact of your words?



Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)

7. Do you prefer having a set plan or leaving things open-ended?



8. When it comes to deadlines, do you prefer to complete tasks ahead of time or at the last minute?
